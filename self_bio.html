Madison Avalon Parker is a 22 year old from just outside of Reading Pennsylvania. She is currently a senior working toward her Bachelor's degree in Information Technology from Pennsylvania State University. Prior to that she studied music technology at The University of the Arts in Philadelphia. She graduated from Schuylkill Valley High School in 2018 on the honor roll. Since then, she has been stuck working in retail while attending college.

Hobbies

Outside of work and academics, Maddie enjoys spending time with her kitten, Newt, playing video games, and spending time outdoors (only when it is warm out though, she hates the cold). She also enjoys drawing. Currently she is working on drawing some of the new tattoos that she is looking to get in the future.
